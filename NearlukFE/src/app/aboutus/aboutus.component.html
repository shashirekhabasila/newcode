About Nearluk

know something about us

Tired of searching for Rental Properties? Look no further! Nearluk is a one stop solution for all rental/ lease based transactions. Whether you are an Owner, or a Tenant or even an Agent, we have the right options to ensure your job gets done. Post an Ad for FREE! Or Bid on a property. A registered individual can post an Advertisement for their Commercial or Non Commercial Property, and also search for any properties available near him/ her. Using the most advanced Business Intelligence and Machine Learning algorithms, we automatically show you properties near you depending on your likes and dislikes.

OUR FEATURES

Our Featured Service that we Provide

  • Agent

    An Agent is a person who acts as medium between Owner and Tenant.He connects to Owner and Owner connects to Agent, it goes vice versa.

  • Bidding

    Registered users can bid for a property posted by Owners . Can view the top biddings for a property

  • Compare

    A user can compare two properties that he founds reliable and apt from his viewpoint. The factors that are involved to compare.

    This option helps to store all the users best-rated properties in “Add to Favorites”. A user can add or remove favorites.

    It is a feature where a map will displayed for every chosen property. It shows the route.

  • nearU

    nearU shows the nearby properties located from your current or preferred location.
